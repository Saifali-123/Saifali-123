FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions):

Q1: How do I book a flight on Get2Trip Pvt. Ltd.?

A: Booking a flight is easy! Simply visit our website, enter your travel details, choose from the available options, and complete the payment process.

Q2: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards and other secure online payment options. Details can be found on our payment page.

Q3: Can I make changes to my booking after confirmation?

A: Yes, you can make changes to your booking within the stipulated time frame, subject to airline policies. Some changes may incur additional fees, so please review the terms and conditions.

Q4: How can I contact customer support?

A: Our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach us through the "Contact Us" section on our website or by emailing support@get2trip.com.

Q5: Are there any hidden fees in the pricing?

A: No, we believe in transparency. The prices displayed on our website include all applicable taxes and fees. You won't encounter any hidden charges during the booking process.

Q6: What happens if my flight is canceled or delayed?

A: In the event of a cancellation or delay, our customer support team will assist you in accordance with the airline's policies. We'll do our best to provide alternative options or refunds based on the circumstances.
