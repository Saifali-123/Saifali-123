Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

Q1: What is Umrah, and how is it different from Hajj?

A1: Umrah is a pilgrimage to Mecca, similar to Hajj but can be undertaken at any time of the year. While Hajj is obligatory for all Muslims once in their lifetime, Umrah is a recommended, non-mandatory pilgrimage.
